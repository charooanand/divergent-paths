Divergent Paths

A New Perspective On Earnings Differences Between Black And White Men Since 1940

The second half of the 20th century began with progressivist hopes for racial equality – or at least a march towards it. But did legislative gains translate to economic advancement?
Bayer & Charles (2018) revise current understanding on the Black-White earnings gaps in the US. By looking beyond simple differences in average incomes, their analysis paints a richer picture of income inequality.
